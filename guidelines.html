Critical

Identity Verification

All walking buddies must complete comprehensive identity verification including government ID, background check, and address verification.

  • Government-issued photo ID
  • Police background check (12 months)
  • Proof of address
  • Reference verification
Critical

Meet in Public

Always meet your walking buddy in well-lit, populated public areas. Never meet at private residences or isolated locations.

‚úì Recommended locations:
  • Community centers
  • Shopping mall entrances
  • Popular park entrances
  • Transit stations
Important

Stay Connected

Maintain communication with trusted contacts and use our in-app safety features throughout your walk.

  • Share your route with a friend
  • Enable location sharing
  • Carry a charged phone
  • Know emergency contacts
Recommended

Timing Guidelines

Plan your walks during appropriate hours and communicate clearly about duration and expectations.

Daylight hours: Preferred for first meetings
Evening walks: Well-lit areas only
Duration: 30-90 minutes recommended
Important

Communication

Maintain respectful, clear communication and establish boundaries early in your walking partnership.

  • Use SafeSpace messaging system
  • Confirm meetings 2 hours prior
  • Respect personal boundaries
  • Report inappropriate behavior
Critical

Trust Your Instincts

If something feels wrong, trust your gut. You have the right to end any walk or interaction at any time.

üö® Red flags: Pressure to change location, inappropriate comments, refusing to verify identity, making you uncomfortable
